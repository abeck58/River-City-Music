Membership of River City Music Community costs $20 per year. Band fees are $100 per school term, billed at the start of each term.

You can play in both bands for the same cost!

Reduced fees may be available for some members. Students are 1/2 price.  Disabled members of Musical Chairs who are also members of The Sporting Wheelies Association get half their fees paid by Sporting Wheelies as a grant this year.

River City Music welcomes new players. We offer you the opportunity to put your skills into practice, with a group of like minded players, under the direction of a professional conductor.
